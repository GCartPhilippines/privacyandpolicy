Privacy & Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Shukiyo Tech (the developer) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data from our applications: GCart, GCart Merchant(GCart Merchant), and GCart Rider (GCartRider). By using any of these applications, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Scope of Data Collection

We collect personal information through account registration, orders, deliveries, communications, and support interactions within the app.

3. Privacy for Customers

Data Collected

  • Name, phone number, and email
  • Delivery addresses and GPS location during orders
  • Order and transaction history

Purpose

  • Order processing and delivery coordination
  • Preventing fraudulent and suspicious activities
  • Handling customer reports, complaints, and order disputes, including investigation and appropriate action when necessary

Shared With

  • Assigned riders for delivery
  • Merchants for order preparation
  • Payment partners when necessary

4. Privacy for Riders

Data Collected

  • Personal identity

Purpose

  • Identity verification and delivery assignment
  • Navigation, tracking, and safety
  • Earnings computation and payouts
  • Preventing fraudulent and suspicious activities

5. Privacy for Merchants

Data Collected

  • Store and owner information
  • Business documents and permits
  • Product listings and pricing

Purpose

  • Store verification and listing
  • Order fulfillment and coordination
  • Payout processing and performance tracking
  • Order processing
  • Preventing fraudulent and suspicious activities

6. Payments

We do not store sensitive financial credentials such as card numbers or e-wallet login details. Payments are currently processed through Cash on Delivery (COD). We retain only transaction records (such as order ID, amount, and status) for accounting, auditing, and dispute resolution purposes.

7. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Use of encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure servers and restricted access
  • Authentication and authorization controls

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational requirements

Requests can be sent to: shukiyotechnology@gmail.com.

We may require verification of identity before processing requests.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After this period, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Data & Account Deletion Requirement

If you wish to delete your account (GCart, GCart Merchant, or GCart Rider) and all associated personal data held by Shukiyo Tech, you do not need the app installed. Please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to: shukiyotechnology@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address or phone number associated with your account.

Upon verification, Shukiyo Tech will securely delete your account credentials and personal data from our active databases within 30 days, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated policy.
